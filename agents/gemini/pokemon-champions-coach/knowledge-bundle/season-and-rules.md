# Current season & rules

Current regulation: **M-B** (Regulation Set M-B), active 2026-06-17 to 2026-09-02. references/ last refreshed 2026-07-18.

Second regulation set of Pokemon Champions and the second "Mega format": every species legal in M-A stays legal, plus 22 newly-recruitable species and 16 new Mega Evolutions (across 15 species — Raichu gets both Mega Raichu X and Mega Raichu Y). Also the format used for the 2026 World Championships during this window.

## Format
- Level: 50, auto-leveled: True
- Win condition: Opponent's active battle team (4 Pokemon) all faint, or in-game timer expires (then it's decided by remaining HP/Pokemon count).
- Team preview: 90s
- Turn timer: 45-60 (source-dependent, verify in-game), match limit: 7-10 (source-dependent, verify in-game) min
- Singles: 1 battler(s) per side
- Doubles: 2 battler(s) per side -- The primary competitive/VGC-style format. Bring a Battle Team of 6, bring 4 into the actual battle (team preview first).

## Team building
- Battle team size: 6, brought to battle: 4
- Species clause: No two Pokemon on a team may share a National Pokedex number. Alternate forms of the same base species (e.g. Heat Rotom + Wash Rotom) still count as the same species and are not allowed together.
- Item clause: No two Pokemon on a team may hold the exact same item (including duplicate Mega Stones).
- Mega Evolution: allowed, max 1 per battle -- A team may carry more than one Mega Stone, but only one Pokemon may actually Mega Evolve per battle.

## Legality
- No separate ban list -- legality is simply "has this species been added to the game yet."
- Excluded: Non-final evolution stages (unevolved Pokemon of any line are not battle-usable at launch)
- Excluded: Box legendaries
- Excluded: Paradox Pokemon
- Excluded: Treasures of Ruin
- Excluded: Restricted/transfer-only species not yet added to the game
- Roster size: 228 base species per community tallies; references/pokemon/ holds 232 files because it also splits out battle-relevant variants (regional forms, Rotom appliances, Lycanroc time forms, Meowstic/Basculegion gender, Paldean Tauros breeds) that some tallies fold into a single count.

## New in M-B
- 22 newly legal species: Vileplume, Qwilfish, Overqwil, Sceptile, Blaziken, Swampert, Mawile, Metagross, Staraptor, Musharna, Scolipede, Scrafty, Eelektross, Pyroar, Malamar, Barbaracle, Dragalge, Grimmsnarl, Falinks, Houndstone, Gholdengo, Annihilape
- 16 new Mega Evolutions across 15 species.
  Champions-exclusive (no mainline equivalent): Staraptor, Scolipede, Scrafty, Eelektross, Pyroar, Malamar, Barbaracle, Dragalge, Falinks, Raichu (X), Raichu (Y)

## Ranked ladder
- Tiers: Beginner, Poke Ball, Great Ball, Ultra Ball, Master Ball, Champion (4 (lowest) to 1 (highest) sub-ranks each)
- Promotion: Win fills the rank gauge 1/4; loss drains it 1/4. Once you promote out of a tier you can no longer fall back into it.
- Gating: Master Ball ranks 3-1 and Champion tier unlock one week into the season, so day-one players cannot rush to top rank immediately.
- Singles and doubles ladders independent: True
